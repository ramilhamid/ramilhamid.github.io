Lambda Spa Center

Lambda Hotel & Jacuzzi Suites features 20 spacious guest rooms and bathrooms that combine comfort, style, elegance and the latest technology.

The Lambda Spa Center with years of experience in the field, is now the epitome of relaxation, detoxication, invigoration, purification, rejuvenation and general well-being.
